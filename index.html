Nykaa is an Indian retailer of beauty, wellness, and fashion products.
Falguni Nayar, a former managing director at Kotak Mahindra Capital Company, founded Nykaa in 2012.
It was launched as an ecommerce portal curating a range of beauty and wellness products.
The brand name Nykaa is derived from the Sanskrit word nayaka, meaning ‘one in the spotlight’.
In 2015, the company expanded from online-only to an omnichannel model and began selling fashion products.
